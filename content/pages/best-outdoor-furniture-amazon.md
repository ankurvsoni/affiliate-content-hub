## Quick verdict

Outdoor furniture lives outside — weather resistance matters more than aesthetics. Powder-coated steel and aluminum hold up better than cheap iron; solution-dyed acrylic fabrics outlast polyester in sun and rain. Measure your space before ordering anything, and factor in how much of the year you'll actually use it — a $200 set you actually sit on beats a $600 set that deteriorates from neglect.

## Who should skip this

Renters on short leases, apartment balconies under 50 sq ft, and anyone in a climate with 8+ months of harsh weather should think carefully — outdoor furniture requires space to store, protect, or leave out, and the cost-benefit only works when you'll actually use it.

## Common mistakes

- Ordering without measuring — outdoor sets are often larger than photos suggest; always check listed dimensions
- Ignoring cushion care — most outdoor cushions are water-resistant, not waterproof; leaving them out in rain degrades them fast
- Buying iron furniture in wet climates — powder-coated iron still rusts at the welds over time; aluminum or resin is better for rain

## Budget tiers

- **Premium ($800+):** Aluminum frame sectionals, teak dining sets, or pergola-integrated furniture. Long-term investment for permanent outdoor spaces.

## FAQ

### What's the most durable outdoor furniture material?
Powder-coated aluminum and teak wood are the most durable long-term. Aluminum doesn't rust; teak weathers gracefully without treatment. Resin wicker is durable and low-maintenance for the price.

### Do I need to cover outdoor furniture in winter?
It depends on material and climate. Metal and resin furniture generally tolerates cold; cushions and wicker should be stored or covered. Even durable furniture lasts longer with a cover during off-seasons.

### How do I choose between a sectional and a dining set?
Ask how you use your patio. If you eat outside primarily, get a dining set. If you want to sit and socialize, a conversation set or sectional is better. Many patios benefit from one of each if space allows.
